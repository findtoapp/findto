## üåé Unlocking web searches

Findto is a decentralized web search app with AI.

Findto mission is to make truly free searches and more productive, putting people in control of search algorithms and generative AIs ‚Äî not the other way around.

Findto proposes a new Web search experience: you are on control of multiple powerful sources and get better results.

The centralized Web, where our searches are monopolized, it's a limiting and exhausting experience. Findto is encouraging everyone to rediscover the Web, for a more diverse, healthier and democratic use of them. Where your choices matter.

Findto is free and open source.

## üåà Features

- **Decentralized Search** ‚Äî Quickly search, the same term, on multiple categories and providers.

- **AI Suggestions** ‚Äî Get instant AI suggestions to help autocomplete terms as you type.

- **Privacy Ranking** ‚Äî See the privacy score of each search provider, know how much they are compromised with your data.

- **Trends** ‚Äî Trending topics, contents, events and places nearby you.

- **Accessibility** ‚Äî Changeable layout and theme, color contrast enhanced, keyboard and screen reader navigation.

- **Internationalization** ‚Äî Chose your country for local sources or use global search.

- **Voice search** ‚Äî Use your voice to search anywhere.

- **Visual search** ‚Äî ** COMING SOON ** Use an image to search on supported sources.

- **Social action** ‚Äî ** COMING SOON ** Instead ads, see a social cause to act.

## üîç Sources

The search providers and data from external APIs available on Findto are chosen by the criteria: 1) service popularity 2) commitment with open access 3) respect of privacy and copyright.

These sources are displayed inside the navigable categories, like: `Web`, `AI`, `Social`, `Video`, `Image`, `Audio`, `Academic`, `Local`, `Shopping`, `Jobs`, `Code` and others.

For more technical details, visit the [Wiki](https://github.com/lucasm/findto/wiki).
